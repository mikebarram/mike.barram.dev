date: "2011-02-17"

draft: true
---

The EntityGridView ignores your ```AllowPaging"False"``` in the designer view.

To stop the grid from paging you have to put  
```
GridView1.AllowPaging = false;  
```
in the ```Page_Load```

See here for details:  
[http://community.codesmithtools.com/nettiers/f/16/t/11405.aspx](http://community.codesmithtools.com/nettiers/f/16/t/11405.aspx)
